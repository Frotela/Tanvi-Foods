Our Story

You’re hungry and don’t have the time or energy to cook something delicious; that’s when the frozen foods come into play. They save time and clean up in the kitchen on those busy nights. When you need a quick fix, these are what you can steer toward. It takes out all the guesswork when cooking on the stovetop. And the myth that frozen foods not being delicious is like the squeaky wheel not getting the grease. It’s just impossible.

Talking of which, let us look into it considering an example of frozen corn which bodes well for customers as an item they feel excited and comfortable with as a quality frozen product. When you pick an ear of corn, the sweetness of it will turn into cornstarch because of the sugar inside the corn kernels. This means that over time, freshly picked corn becomes less sweet and chalkier. However, if we look into frozen corn, it doesn’t have the same problem and you can rely on it consistently. In this pandemic, the demand for these foods has increased drastically which gives ample time for people to explore other things.

Tanvi Foods India Ltd is one of the growing food processing company incorporated in 2007, primarily operating in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Karnataka. The company distributes, trades and process A grade food and beverages in the southern region.

Tanvi foods have grown organically and inorganically, bringing nutrition and freshness into the families who have put their trust into the brand. Today, Tanvi Food co-operates over three cold room facilities stretching up to 8278sq.ft, one food processing and packaging unit of 495 sq. yards and operates over four exclusive points of sales with its subsidiaries. Tanvi Food acknowledges around 24 logistics vehicles dedicated towards F&B distribution making it one among the few selected Growth Oriented and Organized players in this highly unorganized segment.


Tanvi foods focus on providing healthy and fresh food products for variety of customers and consumers. The products are branded as Frozen Kings and sold in retail stores in order to meet foot with time.
